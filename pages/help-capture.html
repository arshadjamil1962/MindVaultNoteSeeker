Mind Vault - Note Seeker your personal assistant chrome extension for securely storing, organizing, and finding your thoughts, knowledge, and resources, all in one place.


Mind Vault Notes Registeration

Imagine you're browsing your favorite website and come across a brilliant idea or important information.

With Mind Vault Note Seeker, capturing your important information is as simple as highlighting the text, right-clicking, and selecting the "Mind Vault Note Addition" option.

  • An Entry screen opens in a new tab, carrying forward the selected text, the source URL, and the current date automatically.
  • Your job? Just fill out the required details like the Title, Priority, Tags, and Keywords.

  • Adding Tags to Note is simple:
    • Just select a Tag from the list OR
    • Type a Tag in the space provided to narrow down the selection.
    • Typed or selected Tags are added to the Note as button elements with a description and an "x" mark.
    • The "x" mark offers the Remove functionality. Click "x" to remove a Tag from the selection list.
    • Non-existing newly added Tags will be saved to the Tags Master Table when the Note is saved.
  • Adding Keywords to Note is also simple:
    • Just select a Keyword from the list OR
    • Type a Keyword in the space provided to narrow down the selection.
    • Typed or selected Keywords are added to the Note as button elements with a description and an "x" mark.
    • The "x" mark offers the Remove functionality. Click "x" to remove a Keyword from the selection list.
    • Non-existing newly added Keywords will be saved to the Keywords Master Table when the Note is saved.
  • To link the current note content with another existing note?
    • Add a cross-reference too—it’s optional but super handy!

  • Once you've filled out the information and are satisfied, just click Save, and your note is securely stored in the database.
  • The Entry screen tab is then closed.

Great! You successfully added your note in your Notes Collection.
